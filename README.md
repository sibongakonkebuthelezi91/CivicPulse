SafeWalk – Women's Safety and Journey Companion App

## Overview

SafeWalk is a mobile application designed to improve the safety of women when traveling, walking, or commuting. The app connects users who are heading to the same destination, provides live safety monitoring, and offers emergency assistance features.

## Problem Statement

Many women feel unsafe when walking alone, especially during early mornings, evenings, or when using public transport and e-hailing services. SafeWalk aims to reduce these risks by creating safer travel experiences through community support, real-time tracking, and emergency response features.

## Features

### 1. Walking Groups
- Creates walking groups for women during morning and evening travel.
- Users can find existing groups or create new groups.
- Automatically creates a group chat when three or more users are traveling to the same destination.
- Prioritizes the user who has the furthest drop-off point to ensure everyone reaches their destination safely.

### 2. Journey Checkpoints
- Tracks user journeys from the moment they leave home.
- Records and analyzes travel patterns.
- Suggests the best departure times based on historical travel data and peak traffic periods.
- Uses checkpoints throughout the journey to monitor progress and safety.

### 3. Guardian Angel Alerts
- If the app cannot find nearby travel companions or groups, it alerts a designated "Guardian Angel" contact.
- Guardian Angels receive notifications and can monitor the user's journey until they reach their destination safely.

### 4. Live Location Sharing
- Shares real-time location with trusted contacts while using e-hailing services.
- Allows friends, family members, or guardians to track the journey live.

### 5. Emergency SOS Group Chat
- Integrates with WhatsApp group chats.
- A panic button sends a silent SOS alert to the emergency group.
- Provides immediate notification without requiring the user to make a call.

### 6. Daily Safety Notifications
- Sends push notifications every morning.
- Reminds users to activate safety features before leaving home.

### 7. Emergency Voice Recording
- Automatically records emergency voice notes during distress situations.
- Stores recordings securely in the cloud for future access and evidence.

## Target Users

- Women commuting to work or school.
- Women using public transportation.
- Women using e-hailing services.
- Individuals seeking additional personal safety measures while traveling.

## Benefits

- Reduces the risk of traveling alone.
- Encourages community-based safety.
- Provides quick access to emergency assistance.
- Keeps trusted contacts informed of user locations.
- Creates a record of incidents through voice recordings and journey logs.

## Future Enhancements

- AI-powered risk detection and route recommendations.
- Real-time crime hotspot alerts.
- Wearable device integration.
- In-app emergency calling and reporting.

## Vision

To create a safer environment where women can travel confidently, knowing they are supported by a trusted community, intelligent safety tools, and real-time emergency assistance.
